About Me

Hi Everybody, it is so nice to meet you all my name is Jack Hogan. I am 31 years old, a typical mad Liverpool Football Club supporter sice birth and I am aspiring to become a Full Stack Engineeer.

I grew up in Liverpool and never knew what I wanted to do with my life as I always thought I would go in the army like most of the men in my family. I served in the forces for just under 8 years and when I came out I tried to become a Personal Trainer, I tried to do Forklift Driving I even tried Cold Calling but never really found any of it interesting. I finally found something that interested me in working for Openreach where I quickly progressed to a Telecommunications Engineer. I spent years working for internet companies helping people doing this but it wasnt quite the final thing for me even though I knew I was close.

I started speaking to an old friend of mine who told me to look at becoming an full stack engineer telling me about the benefits and how much he has done
well for himself doing it so far so I looked into it and realised this would be my next step up to what I was aiming for in my life.
